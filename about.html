About Advance Health International (AHI)

Avance Health International is a leading company in the medical device import and marketing sector in Bangladesh. Specializing in high-quality medical devices, we are committed to improving healthcare standards across the nation. Our product range includes essential healthcare equipment such as glucometers, digital thermometers, digital blood pressure monitors, insulin syringes, insulin pens, IV cannulas, catheters, blood bags, gloves, and various pathological instruments.

We partner with world-renowned manufacturers to import and distribute these critical medical products, ensuring that healthcare providers in Bangladesh have access to the best tools available for patient care. At Avance Health International, we prioritize quality, innovation, and reliability in every product we offer, aiming to make a positive impact on healthcare delivery in the country.

Our Mission

To improve healthcare in Bangladesh by providing high-quality, reliable, and innovative medical devices, ensuring healthcare professionals have access to the best tools for patient care.
